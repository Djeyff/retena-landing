FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How does Retena connect to my WhatsApp groups?+
Add Retena's WhatsApp number to any existing group as a participant. Retena instantly starts capturing and transcribing all voice notes. No apps, no config. Setup is under 2 minutes.
Does my team need to install anything?+
No. Your team keeps using WhatsApp exactly as they always have. Retena is invisible — it just listens in the background. Only the manager/admin needs to access the Retena dashboard.
What happens to the audio files?+
Audio is processed in real-time and immediately deleted. We never store voice recordings — only the text transcription, encrypted at rest. Never used to train AI models.
What languages does Retena support?+
99 languages with automatic detection. Each voice note is detected and transcribed in its own language. Mixed-language teams work perfectly — Spanish, French, Portuguese, Arabic, English, German, and 93 more.
Is Retena legal to use?+
Yes. Retena appears as a visible participant in the group — your team can see it's there. We recommend informing your team. We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and local data protection laws.
Can I try Retena before paying?+
Yes. Every plan includes a 7-day free trial with no credit card. Early access members lock their rate forever.
Why per team, not per seat?+
Because your whole team benefits — not just the person paying. A team of 15 shouldn't pay 15 × $19/seat. Retena scales with the number of groups you manage, not headcount.
How do team seats work?+
Each seat is a separate login to your shared workspace. Add Retena's WhatsApp number to your business groups once — all team members can then search, view summaries, and access transcripts from their own dashboard. Each member gets their own Personal Inbox, notification preferences, and starred items.
Does each team member see all groups?+
No — each seat automatically shows only the groups that person belongs to on WhatsApp. When a team member verifies their phone number, Retena matches it to their WhatsApp groups. Admins can see all groups and can manually grant access to additional groups if needed.
Can I add more seats to my plan?+
Yes. Pro and Business plans can add extra seats for $15/mo each. Contact us or upgrade directly from your dashboard settings.
How fast is the transcription?+
Transcribed in seconds. A typical 1-minute voice note comes back in about 2 seconds. Even a 10-minute recording takes only about 6 seconds. We use military-grade speech recognition optimized for speed and accuracy across 99 languages.
What's the difference between Retena and VozClara?+
VozClara is personal: you transcribe YOUR OWN voice notes. Retena is for teams: it captures EVERYONE's voice notes in WhatsApp groups. Different tools, different use cases — many users have both.
What is VozClara?+
VozClara is personal AI voice transcription inside WhatsApp and Telegram. Send a voice note, get an instant transcript. 99 languages, AI summaries. Free to start — all Retena members get free VozClara Basic. voz-clara.com →
Can Retena capture my 1-on-1 WhatsApp chats?+
No — and that's by design. Retena only captures groups where its number has been added as a member. It has zero access to your personal conversations. For personal voice notes, forward them to Retena's number and they'll appear in your Personal Inbox on the dashboard, transcribed and searchable.
Can I choose which groups Retena captures?+
Absolutely. You have full control. Add Retena's number to a group and capture starts. Pause any group from your dashboard. Remove Retena's number from the group to fully disconnect. You decide exactly which groups are captured.
What's the difference between VozClara and Retena in a group?+
VozClara replies with transcripts directly in the group chat — everyone sees them. Retena captures silently and sends everything to a private dashboard. For business groups, Retena is the right choice because it doesn't clutter your chat with bot replies.
Can I use Retena for personal voice notes too?+
Yes! Forward any voice note from your 1-on-1 WhatsApp chats to Retena's number. It appears in your Personal Inbox — transcribed, searchable alongside your group data. No extra charge, counts against your voice minutes.
What's the difference between Retena and VozClara?+
VozClara gives you instant text back in your chat — great for quick personal transcription. Retena silently captures your business groups AND lets you forward personal notes into a unified searchable dashboard with AI. VozClara for quick replies, Retena for organized memory.
