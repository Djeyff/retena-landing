Security

How we protect your team's communication data.

🔐

Encrypted in Transit

All data is transmitted over TLS 1.3. No plaintext communication, ever.

🗄️

Encrypted at Rest

Your stored transcriptions and messages are encrypted at rest using AES-256.

🏢

Isolated Workspaces

Each organization's data is logically isolated. No cross-tenant data access is possible.

🎙️

No Audio Retained

Voice recordings are transcribed and immediately deleted. We never store raw audio.

🔑

Access Controls

Strict role-based access. Our team cannot read your data without explicit authorization.

📋

Audit Logs

All access to your workspace is logged. Business tier customers can request access logs.

Infrastructure

Retena runs on enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure hosted in the European Union and USA. Our servers are protected by:

  • DDoS protection and rate limiting at the network edge
  • Automated vulnerability scanning and dependency monitoring
  • Regular security patches and updates
  • Database backups with point-in-time recovery, encrypted and stored off-site
  • Isolated network environments — application, database, and cache tiers are separated

Authentication

  • Dashboard access requires phone-based OTP verification — no passwords to steal
  • Session tokens are cryptographically random and expire after 30 days
  • API access uses short-lived tokens scoped to specific operations
  • No third-party OAuth means no risk from compromised social login providers

AI Processing

When processing voice notes and generating AI responses, your data is sent to our AI infrastructure over encrypted connections. We specifically choose providers whose terms prohibit using customer data for model training. Your team's conversations are not used to train any AI model.

Employee Access

Retena employees do not have routine access to customer data. Access requires multi-factor authentication, is granted on a need-to-know basis, and every access event is logged. We will never sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Responsible Disclosure

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly. We take all reports seriously and aim to resolve critical issues within 48 hours.

📧 security@retena.app — Please include a description, steps to reproduce, and potential impact. We will acknowledge your report within 24 hours.

We ask that you do not publicly disclose the issue until we have had the opportunity to investigate and remediate.

Questions

Have security questions or concerns? Contact us or email security@retena.app.
